K I S A

USC's premier Korean International Student Association

About Us

Founded in 1987 as the official Korean student association, we pride ourselves on having the longest history among numerous Korean organizations. We have 50+ members every semester, and we hold a variety of events from tailgating, sports, and retreats to house parties and clubbing events.

What We Do

Members

KISA is home to 50+ students, each with their own backgrounds, studies, and career pursuits. Our Executive Board consists of 8–10 members that take on the responsibility of planning and facilitating KISA events. We also have a long history of alumni to help guide our members.

Meet Us

Recruitment

The opportunities for success are never-ending at KISA. You will be able to meet lifelong friends and make valuable connections that can help you through your academic and career journey. We accept applications every semester, and we strongly encourage you to apply!
